Norwegian Exchange (2023)

My 7 month expedition to the Norwegian coast side allowed me to grow, understand myself and fine-tune my values while broadening my worldview and intercultural skills. Experiencing a life so different from my own has developed my communication, independence and creativity in a way no other experience has.

Also it was ridiculously fun! Going hiking, snowboarding and exploring was an adventure like no other. Highlights include hiking through the Lofoten islands, polar swims in the freezing fjords, paragliding around Bergen, hiking a marathon in a day, seeing the northern lights, and of course, the uncountable number of friends I made along the way.
